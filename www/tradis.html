Product
Placement Guide

The TARDIS Product Placement Guide gives guidelines and rules that must be followed when storing items in your restaurant. These are the rules that the TARDIS team follows when packing your stockroom and will be useful should you wish to make any amendments.

Manager’s Guide

The TARDIS Manager’s Guide was distributed to each restaurant manager at the initial roll out of TARDIS. The guide has step by step instructions on using TARDIS as well as re-sequencing counts and how to get the full benefits of TARDIS.

Product Index

When TARDIS was first deployed in your restaurant, you will have received a Product Index Tool which helped manage your new stockroom layout. This tool creates on page with location information. It also creates an easy update sheet for helping with re-sequencing your counts.
